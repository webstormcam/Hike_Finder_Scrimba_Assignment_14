Ulriken

Length: 3.5km - Est. 2 h 30 m

Ulriken is the highest of the Seven Mountains that surround the city of Bergen, Norway. It has a height of 643 meters (2,110 ft) above sea level.

Length: 4.3km - Est. 1 h 25 m
